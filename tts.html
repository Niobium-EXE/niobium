How to use TTS!

Just use the chat and it will pick a random voice

Some words trigger sound effects too, list below.

If you have a sound effect in your message, for example the middle,
it will play the first part tts, then the sound effect, then the last bits tts.

If you would like to use a specific voice put it in square brackets before your message

For example "[Epic Trailer Guy] In a world..."
As of 4/15/25 all voices are from eleven labs
