How to use TTS!

Just chat and it will pick a random voice from edge tts

Some words trigger sound effects too, list below.

If you have a sound effect in your message, for example in the middle,
it should play the first part tts, then the sound effect, then the rest of the tts.

If you would like to use a specific voice put it in square brackets before your message

For example "[ana] twinkle twinkle little star"

Buttons below will play sound effects or a voice sample
