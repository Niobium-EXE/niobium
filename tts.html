How to use TTS!

Just use the chat and it will pick a random voice

Some words trigger sound effects too Ill add a list on here as they are discovered.

If you would like to use a specific voice put it in square brackets before your message

For example "[Epic Trailer Guy] In a world..."

As of 4/12/25 all voices are from eleven labs
